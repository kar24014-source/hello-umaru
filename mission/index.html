MISSION STATEMENT

BRIGHAM YOUNG UNIVERSITY-IDAHO

Brigham Young University-Idaho was founded and is supported and guided by The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.

The university does this by:

  1. Building testimonies of the restored gospel of Jesus Christ.
  2. Providing a high-quality education.
  3. Serving as many students as possible.
  4. Delivering education that is affordable.
